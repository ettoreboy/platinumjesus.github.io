Forgetme tries to answer all of your most paranoid questions regarding your online life. Which of your accounts are public on the Internet? How easy is to be deleted?

Forgetme uses Pipl free API to find your email and/or name, then it cross-references the most probable results with justdeleteme folder of accounts, rated by privacy policies of the service provider.
